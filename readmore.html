Early Life

Shreya Ghoshal was born on 12 March 1984. Ghoshal began learning music at the age of four.At the age of six, she started her formal training in classical music. When she reached sixteen, she was noticed by filmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali's mother after she won the television singing reality show Sa Re Ga Ma. Following the success, she made her Bollywood playback singing debut with Bhansali's romantic drama Devdas (2002).

Personal Life

On 5 February 2015, Ghoshal married her childhood friend Shiladitya Mukhopadhyaya a traditional Bengali ceremony. According to Ghoshal, apart from being a singer, she loves to travel and read books but it's cooking that has a healing effect on her.On 22 May 2021, Ghoshal gave birth to a baby boy named Devyaan in Mumbai.
